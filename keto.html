Welcome to the kickass meal plan for get you into Ketosis.

This plan will walk you through two weeks, both weekdays and weekends, to get you into ketosis within two weeks.

Keep in mind that Ketosis is a nutrition phase, where your body is burning your stored fats to generate energy that can be used for function. This means that if you eat a cheat meal or go off the rails on a Day 10 or 11, your body will not enter ketosis.

As always, Meal prep is king. Remember, failure to prepare means to prepare for failure.

If you haven't already, go check out my YouTube video[link] where I break down how to prepare a week's worth of meals on the weekend.

Let's get started!
